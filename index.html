Team vision

What we believe

Pushing forward the advancement the technologies and changing the world should be one of the most important goals for us to strive for. École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) thus gathered a team of passionate professors and students of diverse backgrounds to create a Hyperloop pod in response to the initiatives brought by the Hyperloop Competition.

Hyperloop Competition

At EPFL, professor Marcel Jufer first proposed a futuristic transport metro network that operates at a high speed around 500 km/hour inside low-pressure tunnels.
