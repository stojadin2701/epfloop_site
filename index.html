Team vision

Pushing forward the technology and changing the world should be one of the most important reasons to live for each of us. École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) thus gathered a team of passionate professors and students of diverse backgrounds to create a Hyperloop pod in response to the initiatives brought by the Hyperloop Competition.
